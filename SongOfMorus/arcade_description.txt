Song of Morus - Thumby version

Small boss fight game between a young Taoist Priestess and a demon boy. Move up and down to dodge enemy bullets. Auto shoot.

Song of Morus is my personal project series. It's a set of  world setting and art design for me to experiment different ideas. As I recently get a Thumby, i decide to make a Thumby game for this series.
